Short introduction

Jönköping is a city in southern Sweden situated on the southern shore of the country's second largest lake - Vättern, in the providence of Småland. Historically the city has been known for being a popular trading hub for traders moving between the provinces of Östergötaland and Västergötaland since it's geographical position naturally divides the two provinces. Even today a common stereotype about inhabitants of Småland is that they are extremely mindful about economy (i.e cheap).

So, what can you do here?

Take a stroll around the city, Jönköping is a city ever expanding. Take a look at the architecture emerging around the city or visit the historic and world-famous tändsticksområdet.
