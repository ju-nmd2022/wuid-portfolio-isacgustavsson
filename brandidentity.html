This was my final school project for the Fundamentals of graphic design course where my assignment was to create a brand identity style guide for an imagined gin company from Sweden. This is the front and back cover. I made it to look like the colors wrap around the book to close in at the logotype.

In the assignment i was given a few points that i had to follow for the final presentation. It had to include guidelines for logotype, typography and color usage for the brand.

I made up a story about how the brand was created and the co-founders behind it.

I created a logotype for the brand and guidelines for how it should and shouldn't be used.

I made up a color palette and chose two different typefaces to be used in connection to the imagined company.

The last content i put within the brand identity was a mood board displaying the brand colors together with some photography.
